Supercube Centers

A nice Cube Simulator which includes the Original Cube and Supercube in all sizes from 2x2 to 20x20 is Gabbasoft.





The Supercube can be solved normally, however, at the end of the solve the centers will be twisted the wrong way, the following shows how to fix them.

Press Play on the first cube to half-twist the centers on the front & top faces. Press Play on the second cube to half-twist the centers on the front & back faces. The same sequences can also be used to quarter-twist the two centers (on moves 3 & 7) but they must be turned in opposite directions.
